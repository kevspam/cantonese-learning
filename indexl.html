🎯 Speak better Cantonese · tone & pronunciation guide

  • 🎵 6 tones (Jyutping numbers) — 1: high flat (si1), 2: high rising (si2), 3: mid level (si3), 4: low falling (si4), 5: low rising (si5), 6: low level (si6).
  • 🗣️ Common initials: ‘c’ like ts, ‘z’ like ds, ‘ng’ nasal sound. Example: ngo5 (I), nei5 (you).
  • 💡 Polite particles: laa1 (啦), me1 (咩), aa3 (呀) — add friendliness.
  • 🔊 Tap any card to hear real pronunciation. Try to repeat exactly with same tone.
  • ✅ Quick tip: When asking questions, raise your tone at the end — nei5 hou2 maa3? (How are you?)
