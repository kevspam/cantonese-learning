🎵 The 6 Cantonese Tones

Master tones to speak naturally — same sound, different pitch = different word!

How to read the tone colours on every card

Every syllable on every vocabulary card is colour-coded by its tone number. The small coloured bar under each syllable shows its tone. Use the legend at the top of any vocab page as a quick reference.

T1 High Flat ─ T2 High Rising ↗ T3 Mid Flat ─ T4 Low Falling ↘ T5 Low Rising ↗ T6 Low Flat ─
